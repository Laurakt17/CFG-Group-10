Welcome to Belfast!


Belfast is a vibrant cultural hub on Northern Ireland's Eastern coast. Set

on the banks of the River Lagen, the beautiful city of Belfast is the capital

city of Northern Ireland. Packed with lively pubs, an eclectic food scene,

iconic buildings, boutique shopping and a variety of immersive

experiences, you can delve into history, science, literature and more.

Read on for our favourite spots and top tips!

Hidden Gems!

Tetto Belfast

Tetto is a bright, modern, design-led rooftop restaurant and bar, serving amazing Italian food.

Berts Bar & Restaurant

Take a seat in Belfast's only dedicated jazz bar with a French bistro style menu.

Yügo

Yügo serves Asian fusion seafood & vegan plates alongside classic cocktails.

Linen Hall

The Linen Hall is the oldest library in Belfast, pop in for a read or to enjoy lunch in the cafe.

Queens Arcade

Built in 1880, Queen’s Arcade is the last Victorian Arcade in the city. It is home to many luxury Boutiques.

Titanic Belfast

Titanic Belfast is a world-leading visitor attraction which tells the story of RMS Titanic.

Hazelbank Park

The remnants of an estate house. You can walk or cycle to the park from the city center.

Angel & Two Bibles

Buzz for entry and climb the stairway to this hidden speakeasy cocktail bar.

Banana Block

Living museum and events space within a historic linen mill.
